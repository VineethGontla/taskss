The Water Front.

The Water Front Restaurant is a premier fine dining restaurant with the most breathtaking locations. Listed as one of the 50 must-dos in Hyderabad (Source-WOW Magazine). The Water Front Restaurant is on the banks of the Hussain Sagar Lake offers you a picturesque experience in the truest sense of the word with Great Food, Grand Ambience and an Unmatched view. Renowned for its exquisite setting, beautiful decor and seamless service. The Water Front Restaurant serves a wonderful classical Hakka Chinese, Indian and old world Continental cuisine using only the finest, freshest ingredients. It has a well stocked bar with more than 50 plus brands of globally imported wines/spirits/liquors and large variety of cocktails & mocktails. The 'Deck Dining' under the stars at The Water Front Restaurant offers an unsurpassed setting for private dining, special events or corporate dinners with breathtaking views overlooking Hussain Sagar and Buddha Statue
